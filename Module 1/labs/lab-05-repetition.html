My Turn (here's where I wrote the pseudocode):

Scenario:

It seems like it won't stop snowing until June!


Pseudocode:
BEGIN
  WHILE not June
    snow daily
  ENDWHILE
END

Now it's your turn! Your Output (here's where YOU write the pseudocode):

For each one of these scenarios, write the pseudocode. Each of your examples should look just like mine. 

Scenario:

I'm not going to get a good night's sleep while I have kids in the house.


Pseudocode:
BEGIN
  WHILE kidsInHouse
    badNightOfSleep
  ENDWHILE
END


Scenario:

I couldn't breathe when she was in the room. Her perfume was so strong!

Pseudocode:
BEGIN
  WHILE shesInRoom
    cantBreathe
  ENDWHILE
END


Scenario:

I have 10 apples and I'm going to give everyone one apple until I run out.

Pseudocode:
BEGIN
  WHILE apples is less than 10
    Display remaining apples
    subtract 1 from apples
  ENDWHILE
END
